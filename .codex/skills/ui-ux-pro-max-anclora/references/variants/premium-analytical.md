# Variant: Premium Analytical

Use when the surface should express premium intelligence through analysis.

Reference:

- `Anclora Data Lab`

## Protect

- readable signal
- premium analytical framing
- disciplined filter and card grammar
- density that helps, not overwhelms

## Prioritize

- information hierarchy
- chart and metric legibility
- structured shells
- curated premium tone

## Watch for

- generic BI feel
- decorative chrome that harms comprehension
- internal dashboard density with no refinement
