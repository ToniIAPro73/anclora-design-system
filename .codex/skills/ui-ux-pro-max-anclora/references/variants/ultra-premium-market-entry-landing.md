# Variant: Ultra Premium Market-Entry Landing

Use when the target is a public ultra-premium landing whose job is authority and qualified attraction rather than inventory browsing.

Reference:

- `anclora-private-estates-landing`

## Protect

- immediate thesis
- authority without fake stock
- selective attraction of owners, investors, and partners
- visible ecosystem credibility

## Prioritize

- first viewport clarity
- narrative rhythm
- CTA precision
- trust-building blocks over generic marketing sections

## Watch for

- traditional agency-homepage feel
- broad or noisy messaging
- inventory simulation
- conversion mechanics that feel mass-market
