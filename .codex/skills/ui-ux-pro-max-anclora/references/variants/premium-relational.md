# Variant: Premium Relational

Use when the surface must turn interest into selective partnership or curated relationship.

Reference:

- `Anclora Synergi`

## Protect

- trust
- admission clarity
- curated ecosystem feel
- consistency between public and private phases

## Prioritize

- guided forms
- clear process storytelling
- relational warmth with control
- transitions from attraction to workspace

## Watch for

- generic B2B portal feel
- over-administrative UI
- weak sense of curation, status, or belonging
